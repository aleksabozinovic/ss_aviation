About Us

When people go to the theater and watch a production, they focus on the actors performing on stage. Few pay any mind to the backstage crew, costume designers, makeup artists, and lighting specialists behind the curtain. But the truth is, those people are just as important when it comes to delivering an amazing production that wows the audience. Similarly, when people think of air travel, they often picture those at the forefront of an airline company–pilots and cabin crew members. However, there are a host of people operating behind the scenes that make air transport operations possible. We are those people. From cleaning services and bookings to cargo and security, SS Aviation is the manpower behind a well functioning airport.

Our story began in 2020 as an ambitious venture between two ambitious people, and we’re proud to say that that ambition has more than paid off! After just one year of operation, SS Aviation has experienced significant growth and achievement. We attribute our success to technological innovation and an unwavering desire to exude excellence in every facet of our business. Our commitment to serving our clientele is the backbone of SS Aviation. We know that great service means happy guests, and happy guests means a thriving business! We also understand that effectively serving our guests begins with ensuring the highest quality of expertise and experience. In every area of our company, our staff brings their knowledge and proficiency to ensure our customers experience only the joy of travel and never the frustrations associated with travel.

Ultimately, our desire is to ensure an excellent, all-around travel experience for each and every customer in our care.

Company Bio:

SS Aviation is a newly founded company offering top of line airport auxiliary services. We truly do it all–bookings, ticketing, luggage, cargo, security, housekeeping, ground handling, and much more! We ensure all behind the scenes airport needs and expectations are not only met but exceeded. Our mission is to reduce stress and hassle for all travelers to ensure a smooth, problem-free travel experience.
