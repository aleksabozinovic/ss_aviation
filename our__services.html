As a licensed private security agency, we are dedicated to providing you with the highest quality of care in every area of operation. We provide stellar services in the following areas:

  • Housekeeping – Whether in the airport or on an actual airplane, any space under our care is a space you can expect to sparkle and gleam! Our team of housekeepers receive continuous professional training and stay up to date on the latest information to ensure airports and airplanes exceed expectations.

  • Security – Above all, the security and safety of all travelers is our number one priority. Our highly trained security staff offers personalized assistance throughout the entire screening process to eliminate any fear or confusion regarding security compliance.

  • Passengers – We want our guests to be safe, informed, and comfortable from the moment they walk through our doors to the moment they board their planes. That’s why we provide assistance with ticketing and passports, making sure all necessary documentation is accounted for. We also keep our clients up to date regarding all flight related information and inform them of any changes to their flight. We assist with luggage and security checkpoints, ensuring our customers are guided and assisted throughout the entire process. Special care and attention is given to elderly people, single women traveling with children, people with disabilities, and anyone with a health condition.

  • Bookings – Booking a ticket is the first step in air travel. To best serve our clientele, we ensure tickets are booked on time and at the lowest possible rates.

  • Facility Management – We use modern technology and advanced tools to maintain and repair all airport buildings and equipment. Our team cares about both safety and aesthetics when it comes to facility management.

  • Ground Handling – When an aircraft is on the ground, there are a variety of necessary services that demand attention to detail and a wealth of expertise. We guarantee speed, efficiency, and accuracy when it comes to providing the following: baggage/cargo services, ramp services, catering services, cabin services, passengers services, and field operation services.

  • Air Cargo – Because air cargo strengthens the global supply chain, it’s a critical task that demands precision and efficiency. We are experts when it comes to air cargo handling processes. We strictly adhere to standard procedures regarding packing, storing, and loading and unloading cargo for transit. Strict supervision and environmental practices ensure cargo is transported with 0% damage.

  • Flights – Our team guarantees best practices when it comes to interpreting and transmitting weather and aeronautical information to pilots before, during, and after flights.

Meet and Assist Services

Air travel can be frustrating. Between tickets, passports, baggage checks, security screenings, and flight information, it’s no wonder people get stressed out at airports! However, with SS Aviation’s meet and assist services, there’s no need for stress. From the moment you walk through our doors, you will be greeted by a member of our team who is eager and excited to help you through the entire preflight process. We ensure all required documentation is accounted for, we guide you through both luggage and security checkpoints, and we guarantee our passengers board the correct flight and depart on time. We also keep you informed of any changes to your flight so that you stay up to date on important information at all times. If you need personal escort services, we cater to those needs as well. People with special needs, single mothers traveling with children, elderly people, and anyone with a health issue will be given special care and attention. We want you to relax and enjoy your travel experience without having to worry about a single thing. Our team at SS Aviation is thrilled to serve you as you travel from one destination to the next.
