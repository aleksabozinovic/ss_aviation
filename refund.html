Refund Policy

The services by SS Aviation shall be subject to the refunds under the following terms. We only accept payment methods that are valid for our services and all refunds will be credited back using the same method of payment. By submitting your order, you authorize us to charge your credit card or account, including taxes, shipping, handling, and any agreed-upon amounts. If we are unable to verify or accept your payment method, your order may be automatically suspended or canceled.

An invoice will be sent to you after the requested services are executed, and it will be considered an official payment request unless otherwise agreed upon. We warrant that services provided to clients will be performed professionally and properly.

If you request it, we will charge your account or credit card for the amount agreed upon under the executed contract or as otherwise agreed upon by the parties. If a flight is delayed at the client's request, we will not be responsible for any costs incurred by the delay unless agreed upon in writing.

We do not offer refunds for hardware, software, maintenance, and support services once agreed upon by the client. If the third party services provider or carrier disputes an invoice in good faith, they must provide written details of the disputed element within 30 days of receiving the invoice. Until resolved, the carrier cannot delay payment of the invoice but can withhold payment of the disputed element.

We reserve the right to change the payment and refund policy terms and conditions at any time, and any such changes will take effect when posted on the website or directly communicated with the client.
